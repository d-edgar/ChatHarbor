ChatHarbor

All your chats, one harbor.

Download for macOS or build from source on GitHub

A lightweight, native macOS app that brings all your messaging services together in a single window. No more switching between tabs.

⚙️

20+ Services

Slack, Teams, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram, and many more — all in one place.

🎨

Themes

Choose from standard and seasonal color themes, each with light and dark mode support.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Fine-grained control over banners, sounds, badges, and per-service muting.

🔒

Workspace Guard

Automatically clears your clipboard when switching between personal and work chats.

👁

Privacy Shield

Automatically blurs your chats when your screen is being shared or recorded.
